About the game

Pandora is a text heavy edutainment game focused on providing puzzles complimented by text. People have to read dialogues and notes in the game to extrapolate the solution to the puzzles and finish the game. The game goes through a week of an apprentice wizard in a plant over growth outbreak. Each day introduces new puzzles and story context and increases the difficulty in addition to the base difficulty selected at the start if the game.
